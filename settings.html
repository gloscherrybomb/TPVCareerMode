Discord Notifications

Your personal Discord channel webhook URL

  1. Open Discord and go to a server you own or manage
  2. Create a private channel for your notifications (or use an existing one)
  3. Right-click the channel and select "Edit Channel"
  4. Go to "Integrations" tab, then click "Webhooks"
  5. Click "New Webhook" and give it a name (e.g., "TPV Career Mode")
  6. Click "Copy Webhook URL" and paste it in the field above

Note: Keep your webhook URL private. Anyone with this URL can send messages to your channel.
