# TPV Career Mode - Beginner's Guide

Welcome to TPV Career Mode! This guide will help you understand the key features and get you started on your virtual cycling career.

## What is TPV Career Mode?

TPV Career Mode is a web-based virtual cycling career game that transforms your TrainingPeaks Virtual (TPV) race results into an immersive progression experience. Think of it as a career mode for virtual cycling - you'll advance through stages, earn awards, develop your personality as a racer, and compete against a diverse AI peloton while racing real events on TPV.

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up & Link Your Account
- Visit the TPV Career Mode website and create an account
- You'll need your TPV User ID (UID) to link your race results
- Your UID can be found in your TPV profile or race results

### 2. Understanding the Season Structure
Season 1 consists of **9 stages** that you'll complete sequentially:
- **5 mandatory stages** - specific events you must complete
- **3 choice stages** - you pick from a pool of optional events (but can only use each once!)
- **1 final stage** - a special finale to cap off your season

You must complete Stage 1 to unlock Stage 2, Stage 2 to unlock Stage 3, and so on.

## Core Features

### üèÅ Race & Progress

**How Racing Works:**
1. Complete races on TrainingPeaks Virtual as normal
2. Your results are automatically processed and added to Career Mode
3. You earn points based on your finishing position
4. Complete the stage to unlock the next one

**Points System:**
- Higher finishing positions = more points
- Beat your predicted position? Earn **bonus points**! (1-5 extra points)
- Points accumulate throughout the season for overall standings

**Predictions:**
The game analyzes the field's ratings to predict where you'll finish. If you perform better than expected, you'll earn bonus points. Example:
- Predicted: 15th place
- Actual: 8th place
- Result: +3 bonus points!

### üéØ Awards & Achievements

Earn awards for exceptional performances:

**Race Performance Awards:**
- **Medals** - Podium finishes (Gold/Silver/Bronze)
- **Punching Above** - Beat your prediction by 10+ places
- **Giant Killer** - Defeat the highest-rated rider in your race
- **Bullseye** - Finish exactly at your predicted position
- **Hot Streak** - Beat predictions multiple races in a row
- Many more based on specific accomplishments

Check your **Trophy Cabinet** (Palmares page) to see all your awards!

### üé§ Post-Race Interviews & Personality

After each race, you'll face a post-race interview with 3 response options. Your choices shape your **Media Personality** across 6 traits:

- **Confidence** - Bold vs. Modest
- **Humility** - Gracious vs. Self-focused
- **Aggression** - Attacking vs. Conservative
- **Professionalism** - Diplomatic vs. Candid
- **Showmanship** - Entertaining vs. Reserved
- **Resilience** - Determined vs. Reflective

**Why does this matter?**
- Your personality influences race narratives and stories
- Unlocks personality-specific upgrades in the store
- Earns you a dynamic persona label (e.g., "The Bold Competitor", "The Quiet Professional")
- Makes your career unique to how you want to "role-play" your racer

**Interview Tips:**
- Questions adapt to your race performance (wins, setbacks, rivalries)
- Each response affects different personality traits
- Be consistent to develop a strong personality in certain traits (70+)
- No "wrong" answers - just shape your character how you want!

### üí∞ Cadence Credits & The Store

**Earning Cadence Credits (CC):**
- Win awards to earn Cadence Credits
- Different awards grant different amounts of CC

**The Store:**
- Purchase upgrades using your Cadence Credits
- Upgrades provide **conditional bonus points** in races
- Example: "Aero Wheels" - Get +6 points if you beat prediction by 5+ AND finish top 10
- Example: "Pace Notes Earbud" - Get +3 points if you finish within ¬±5 places of prediction
- **3 equipment slots** - choose which upgrades to equip for each race

**Upgrade Tiers:**
- 120 CC upgrades (basic)
- 200 CC upgrades (intermediate)
- 300 CC upgrades (advanced)
- 400 CC upgrades (elite, some require high personality stats)

**Strategy Tip:** Save CC for upgrades that match your racing style!

## Tips for Success

### 1. **Strategic Event Selection**
For your 3 choice stages, pick events that:
- Match your strengths (climbing, sprinting, time trials, etc.)
- Give you the best chance for strong finishes and awards
- Help you earn Cadence Credits for store upgrades

### 2. **Maximize Bonus Points**
- Study your prediction each race
- Target finishing better than predicted for those crucial bonus points
- A few bonus points per race adds up over a season!

### 3. **Develop Your Personality Consistently**
- If you want to unlock personality-specific store items (70+ trait required), be consistent in your interview responses
- Or, create a balanced all-rounder persona by varying your responses
- Your personality makes your race narratives more immersive!

### 4. **Smart Store Strategy**
- Don't spend all your CC immediately
- Save for higher-tier upgrades that match your racing style
- Read the conditional bonuses carefully - pick upgrades you can actually trigger
- You only have 3 equipment slots, so choose wisely

### 5. **Check Your Awards**
- Review your Trophy Cabinet regularly
- Identify which awards you're close to earning
- Target specific awards by adjusting your racing approach

### 6. **Explore the Peloton**
- Check out your bot competitors' profiles
- Learn their strengths and weaknesses
- It adds to the immersion when you recognize "rivals"!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I go back and redo events?**
A: No, once you complete an event, it's locked in. This ensures fair competition and progression.

**Q: What happens if I miss a stage?**
A: For mandatory stages, you must complete them to progress. Some stages may have timing requirements, so check the event details carefully.

**Q: Do I have to use all my Cadence Credits?**
A: No! Save them for upgrades you really want. Your CC balance carries through the season.

**Q: Can I change my equipped upgrades between races?**
A: Yes! Visit the store and adjust your equipped items before each race.

**Q: How are predictions calculated?**
A: Based on the field's Event Ratings (ARR). Higher-rated riders are predicted to finish better.

**Q: What's ARR?**
A: Athlete Race Rating - a measure of a rider's ability in the TPV system. Career Mode uses this for predictions.

**Q: Is this affiliated with TrainingPeaks Virtual?**
A: No, TPV Career Mode is an independent community project that uses publicly available race results.
