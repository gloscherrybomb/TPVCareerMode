What are Special Events?

Special Events are challenges that exist outside the regular season calendar. They offer unique opportunities to earn rewards and boost your career.

  • Don't affect Season Standings - Your season progress remains separate
  • Contribute to Career Points - Results count toward your global ranking and career totals
  • Unlock Achievements - Earn unique awards and accolades
  • Race Other Humans - Fixed-window special events allow you to race other humans to unlock Career Mode rewards

Special Events may be unlocked through the store, earned as rewards, or made available to all riders during special occasions.
