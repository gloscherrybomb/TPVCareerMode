How It Works

  • Earn: Get Cadence Credits from awards (podiums, special feats, GC/season awards)
  • Unlock: Purchase upgrades from four tiers (120/200/300/400 CC)
  • Equip: Fit up to 3 slots (buy additional slots with CC)
  • Trigger: All equipped upgrades can activate in one race if conditions are met
  • Cooldown: Upgrades that trigger are unavailable for the next race
  • Bonus: Upgrades add race points only—never affect time, position, or ARR
