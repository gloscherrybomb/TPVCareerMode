About TPV Career Mode

Your journey from local amateur to world tour champion

What is TPV Career Mode?

TPV Career Mode is a community-driven progression system for TrainingPeaks Virtual (TPV) racing. We transform individual race results into an immersive career experience where every pedal stroke counts toward your journey through the ranks of virtual cycling.

Think of it as your virtual cycling career simulator - complete with progressive challenges, competitive standings, awards, and a vibrant peloton of AI-powered competitors who keep the racing exciting from Event 1 through to the season finale.

Season Structure

Event Variety

Season 1 features 9 carefully selected events spanning the full spectrum of virtual cycling:

  • Flat Criteriums: High-speed technical racing
  • Rolling Courses: Punchy hills test your versatility
  • Mountain Stages: Long climbs separate contenders from pretenders
  • Time Trials: Pure power against the clock

Progressive Difficulty

Events are carefully ordered to introduce challenges gradually. Early events establish your baseline, mid-season races test your development, and late-season events crown the champion.

Meet the Peloton

Over 200 AI-powered competitors populate the field, each with:

  • Unique Profiles: AI-generated backstories and images
  • Performance Ratings: ARR-based skill levels from 700 to 1500+
  • Consistent Racing: Deterministic simulation ensures fair, repeatable results
  • Realistic Variation: Even top riders have off days

Whether you're racing against "The Mountain Crusher" Sarah Chen (1450 ARR) or the developing talent of newcomer Mike Johnson (850 ARR), every competitor adds depth to your racing experience.

Standings & Competition

Season-Long Battle

The standings page shows how you stack up against the entire peloton. Thanks to our bot backfilling system, every competitor shows the same number of completed events, making the leaderboard a true measure of performance excellence.

Fair Play

Your position is earned through consistent strong performances. Beat high-rated riders, exceed predictions, and accumulate points through skill - not just participation.

Technical Features

Automatic Results Processing

Race results are automatically processed through our GitHub Actions workflow. Upload a CSV from TrainingPeaks Virtual, and within minutes your profile updates with new points, unlocked events, and earned medals.

Real-Time Predictions

Our prediction algorithm analyzes the field's Event Ratings to forecast finishing positions. Beat your prediction significantly, and you'll earn valuable bonus points.

Secure Authentication

Google authentication provides secure, seamless access to your career. Your TrainingPeaks Virtual UID links your TPV results to your Career Mode profile without requiring your TPV credentials.

Community & Independence

Community Effort

TPV Career Mode is a passion project built by virtual cycling enthusiasts for virtual cycling enthusiasts. We're constantly improving the experience based on community feedback and racing data.

Independent Operation

TPV Career Mode is completely independent from TrainingPeaks and TrainingPeaks Virtual. We use publicly available race results to create an enhanced racing experience, but we are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to TrainingPeaks or TrainingPeaks Virtual in any way.
